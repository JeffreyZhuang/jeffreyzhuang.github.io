Guidance, Navigation, and Controls Testbed

Old prototype when I was 14. Visual-inertial odometry with only a cheap IMU, camera, and Kalman filter running real-time on a Raspberry Pi 4. Assembled out of laser cut wood, carbon fibre tubes, and epoxy resin.

I didn't have a GPS module at that time and a university teacher on an online forum told me about optical flow. Coincidentally, I had a Raspberry Pi, camera, and IMU so I decided to try it myself. Went down the google search rabbit hole, downloaded and printed out a bunch of research papers.

If I did this again, I could do it so much better using what I know now.
